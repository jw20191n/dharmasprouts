This website catalogues and indexes English and Chinese Dharma materials for youth and children to help families and Dharma centers find and use the rich Buddhist materials already available.

This website was created by Tsechen Kunchab Ling under a grant from the Sachen Foundation. Tsechen Kunchab Ling is a Buddhist Temple practicing in the Sakya Tradition of Tibetan Buddhism. It is the seat of Sakya Dolma Phodrang in the Americas. The Sachen Foundation is a non-profit organization under the leadership of His Holiness the 42nd Sakya Trizin. Its mission is to help transform lives and communities through Dharma education and publication.
